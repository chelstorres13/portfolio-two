Welcome! I'm Chelsea

I'm a Software Developer based in Kansas City, MO. I'm currently studying full-stack web development at Thinkful.

I have a diverse educational background including a Bachelor's degree in Interdisciplinary Studies and a minor in Education. Transitioning into software development was an easy choice for me because I love to be constantly learning.

About Me

I love web development because I enjoy solving complex problems and forcing myself to dig deeper when answers aren't readily available. It's refreshing to have a job where new challenges await you everyday.

I look forward to beginning a career where I get to work along side other developers who come from all backgrounds. I enjoy gaining perspective from others through collaboration as well as sharing knowledge when my expertise is needed.

Outside of work I enjoy spending time with my family, reading by a well-lit window, creating digital art, and trying out new recipes in the kitchen.

So far my Software Development toolkit includes Javascript, HTML, and CSS. I'll be learning much more in the coming months and aim to provide full-stack web development services to clients.
